About Dragon's Word



Guide your pet dragon on a word-find quest the likes of which you've never experienced before, and test your vocabulary, strategy, and pattern-finding skills along the way. Spot words, chain words, overlap words, and reuse your most valuable letters to boost your score into the stratosphere.

Dragon's Word features a huge dictionary of words and definitions, adorable art by Minju Chun, a mellow soundtrack, and hours of puzzling pleasure. Sharpen your vocabulary and your mind, all while playing with a cute baby dragon.

You'll have fun; Dragon's Word!
